Placeholders

PlaceholderAPI​

If you got PlaceholderAPI installed you can...

  • ... use the placeholders from PlaceholderAPI on our plugin.
  • ... use our placeholders in other plugins.
info

For our PlaceholderAPI placeholders you will need to replace...

  • VillageDefense
  • BuildBattle
  • MurderMystery
  • TheBridge

...PLUGININDICATOR with villagedefense

...PLUGININDICATOR with buildbattle

...PLUGININDICATOR with murdermystery

...PLUGININDICATOR with thebridge

...ARENAID with the arena you want the placeholder for.
